A Spellcaster archetype that Xyz Summons Xyz Monsters and uses them to contact fuse into Fusion Monsters. Each card deals with returning cards from the field to the hand while also aiming to Set 2-3 backrow cards and end on at least 1 Fusion Monster who gets certain effects based on the Xyz Monster(s) used for its Summon.

Fallen

A Ritual archetype that utlizes its Spells that are banished or in the GY in order to Ritual Summon from the GY while also relying on the value of the "Chapter" in the Spell's name, similar to how Galaxy Satellite Dragon uses the value of the "Number" in Number monsters' names - with a main gameplan being use the "Chapter" Continuous Spells in a manner akin to flipping through the pages of a book by sending "Chapter" Spells you control to the GY to swap them for a banished "Chapter" or to use their effects while also getting as many materials in the GY as possible to dig for going second cards to help break boards and get in for game.

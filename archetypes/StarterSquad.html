EXU: Extinction Unleashed

Starter Squad

Ava

A mid range Deck that doesn't put up too much turn 1 but left alone really punches hard. The Deck's goal is to be able to conquer the board with their Braves, before ending in style by Summoning the Violet Sorceress herself, complete with the ability to bounce a card you control to pop a card during either player's turn.

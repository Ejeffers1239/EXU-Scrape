EXU: Extinction Unleashed

Tempo Warrior

Knightmare

A musical of a Synchro and Xyz archetype meant to mimic the idea of tempo. The pace increases during your turn as plays are made and damage is dealt, making the BPM go up, whereas on your opponent's turn, it slows down as you attempt to stop your opponent from playing and getting in for damage, making the BPM go down, with its supporting cards being based on other musical terms.

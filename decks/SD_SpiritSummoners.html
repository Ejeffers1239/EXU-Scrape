EXU: Extinction Unleashed

Structure Deck: Spirit Summoners

Gatr

Now introducing a brand-new Ritual theme: Genjutsu! These animal spirits are all Ritual monsters with an unusual twist - they can all discard from your hand to Ritual Summon each other! Thanks to this, they don't need to play any Spells or Traps, so you can focus on supporting your Deck with powerful monsters that activate in the hand, such as Ash Blossom & Joyous Spring and the other popular Ghost Girls. Furthermore, once you've summoned your lineup of spiritual creatures, you can use them for Fusion, Synchro, Xyz, and even Link Summons, all of which can be found in this Deck! These cards work well with previous Ritual themes, such as Megalith, Draitron, and Nekroz.

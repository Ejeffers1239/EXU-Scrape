FAQ

Q: The numbers! What do they mean?

A: Total Wins · Total Playcount · Winrate.

Q: How do I interpret this information?

A: In every game we track, if a card appears anywhere besides the hand or deck in a game, it is considered as being “played” in that game. If the player who played that card goes on to win the game, each card they played is awarded a win.

Q: Why are some of the archetypes highlighted in red?

A: There is insufficient data to make any strong claims about how well that Archetype did in ladder. We use the broad metric N ≥ 30 to provide "enough data", but we realize that this may even still be insufficient data.
